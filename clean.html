About

I am a PhD student at Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS). I am co-advised by Krishna P. Gummadi and Manuel Gomez Rodriguez. Before joining MPI-SWS, I completed my undergraduate studies at LUMS School of Science and Engineering, Lahore, Pakistan.

Research interests

  • Fairness, accountability and transparency in machine learning.
  • Measurement, analysis and design of social computing systems.
  • Retrieval of diverse and trustworthy information from online social networks.

Codes, datasets and web-services

  • Fairness in Classification: Code related to our AISTATS’17, WWW’17 and NIPS’17 papers. Provides a Python implementation of fair logistic regression classifiers.

  • What is Happening? Web-service related to our CSCW’16 paper. Discover real-time topical news on Twitter!

  • Dataset for our ICWSM’16 paper.

  • Who is Who? — Discover crowdsourced opinion on Twitter users.

  • Whom to Follow? — Discover topic authorities on Twitter.
